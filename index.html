Delete Account – MatchMyPet

You can delete your account directly in the app:

  1. Open MatchMyPet
  2. Go to Settings / Profile
  3. Select Delete account and confirm

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by email:

bosoninfinity@gmail.com

Please include the email used in the app and the subject Delete My Account.

We process deletion requests within 30 days.

Data deleted

  • User account and profile
  • Authentication identifiers
  • User content related to the account

Data retained

  • Security/legal logs if required by law
